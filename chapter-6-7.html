The chapter progresses, the text introduces key considerations for Speculative Design within an educational context. It emphasizes the agency of the project and the importance of maintaining ambiguity, abstraction, and ambivalence as signals of successful design research. Bratton introduces the concepts of "prefigurative" material politics and advises caution against lapsing into idealistic forms that delay the enforcement of intended outcomes.

Furthermore, the chapter advocates for a balance between means and vision, arguing that sustained and diverse investment in conceptually courageous design, art, and humanities is necessary for realizing the social value of investments in emerging technology. The text concludes with insights on the tactical exceptions to norms granted within Speculative Design, presenting it as a zone where new norms are prototyped with some impunity.
